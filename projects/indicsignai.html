CHAPTER 01 SYSTEM ARCHITECTURE

PIPELINE OVERVIEW

IndicSignAI bridges the communication gap by translating Indian Sign Language (ISL) directly into text in real-time. The architecture is designed to be extremely lightweight, allowing inference on standard CPUs within 45ms.

ZAP!
  • Input Layer: WebGL captures live webcam frames at 30 FPS.
  • Feature Extraction: MediaPipe extracts 3D hand and pose landmarks instantly.
  • Classification: An EfficientNetV2-S model classifies the sequence into one of 178 ISL classes.
  • Translation & NLP: NLLB-200 and T5 Transformer map the glosses to 6 natural Indic languages.
